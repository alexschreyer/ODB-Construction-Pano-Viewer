About

This interactive viewer displays 360-degree panoramas of the Olver Design Building construction on the UMass Amherst campus. All images were taken in summer 2016. More information...

You can select floors/locations at the top of the screen and individual panoramas using the menu at the left. This viewer can also be installed as a web app by selecting "Add to Home Screen" from your browser menu.
